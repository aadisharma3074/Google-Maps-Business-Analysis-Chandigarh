# Google-Maps-Business-Analysis-Chandigarh
Data Analytics Internship Project - Week 3
📍 Google Maps Business Analysis – Chandigarh
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Beauty Salons and Restaurants in Chandigarh using data collected from Google Maps. The goal is to identify businesses with limited digital presence, analyze customer engagement through ratings and reviews, and provide actionable recommendations to improve their online visibility and business performance.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze Google Maps business data.
Evaluate customer ratings and reviews.
Identify businesses with and without websites.
Compare business performance across sectors.
Provide data-driven business recommendations.
Build interactive Power BI dashboards.

💡 Business Insights
Beauty Salon
Many salons rely only on Google Maps and do not have dedicated websites.
Businesses with higher ratings generally receive more customer reviews.
Certain sectors have a higher concentration of salons, indicating strong local demand.
Specialized services attract greater customer engagement.
Restaurants
Several restaurants depend on Google Maps rather than dedicated websites.
Restaurants offering delivery services tend to receive higher customer engagement.
Popular cuisines attract more customer reviews.
Commercial sectors have the highest concentration of restaurants.
